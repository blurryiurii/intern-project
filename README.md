# Intern Doors (Quoridor)
[Wikipedia article on rules & history](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quoridor)

## Rules
General
- 9x9 grid, turn-based
- Goal: **move your player to opposite side before your opponent**
- Every turn, you can either (1) move your player orthogonally or (2) place a wall
- In a 2-player game, you get 10 walls (20 total walls / 2 players)

Walls
- Walls are of length 2 and are placed between two rows or columns of cells
- Once placed, walls cannot be moved or removed. Use them wisely!
- When adjacent to another player, you can jump over that player, but not over a wall
- Walls cannot cut a player's last path to the other side of the board
